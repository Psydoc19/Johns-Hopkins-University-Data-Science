Gapminder Data Exploration

  • Presenter: Vitalii Diakonov
  • Date: 04/03/2024

This presentation introduces a Shiny application designed to explore various global health and economic indicators provided by the Gapminder dataset.

Slide 2: The Gapminder Dataset

  • Global Data: Life Expectancy, GDP per Capita, Population.
  • Insights: Understand global trends over decades.
  • Application: Visualize and analyze country-specific and global patterns.

The Gapminder dataset provides a unique lens through which we can observe the development and health of nations over time.

Slide 3: Shiny Application Features

  • Interactive Exploration: Users can select countries, continents, and time ranges to explore.
  • Dynamic Visualizations: Life expectancy and GDP per capita over time.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for ease of use without sacrificing depth of analysis.

The application aims to make data exploration accessible and informative.
